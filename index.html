F.O.S.A LOAN

ASSET LOANS

Open Asset Finance account today with at least 6 months saving and acquire land or motor vehicle of your choice with flexible affordable monthly payments.
NB: Security Perfection must be done

BUY LAND

Interest charged at 1% p.m fixed.
Repayment period is upto 6 years.
Funding is upto 5 times.
Loan security asset acquired.
Valuation report required during appraisal.

MOTOR VEHICLE

Interest charged at 1% p.m fixed.
Deposit 30% then finance 70% - New car
Deposit 40% then finance 60% - Second hand car.
Maximum Repayment period to 3 years.
• Loan security asset acquired.
Comprehensive insurance cover for motor vehicle.
• Car track installed

KILIMO LOAN

• Funding is upto 5 times savings.
• Interest rate 1%pm fixed.
• Loan security collaterals (logbook, Title deed) 6 months savings

MOBILE LOANS

• Granted after 6 months savings/member should have an active account.
Member must have minimum share capital of KES 2,500
Maximum loan amount is KES 30,000
• Member must have minimum savings of KES 35,000
Interest rate of 7.5% per month
Maximum repayment period of 1 month
• Qualification to be graduated based on member credit score.

B.O.S.A LOAN

Development/Normal loans

Loan granted after 6 months of savings.
Maximum repayment period is 72 months.

For members in groups & cell groups:-
For cell groups minimum membership is 5 and maximum 20.
Interest rate is 1% P/A Fixed.
All loans shall be guaranteed by all members of the group/cell groups.
NOTE: Any loan above Ksh.1 million shall be secured with a title deed/logbook.

For members with BOSA accounts who wish to borrow individually
Interest rate is 1% P/A Fixed.
• All loans must be fully secured with logbook/title deed.
NOTE: Cell groups, Groups/Churches and Bosa individual loans multiplier is 3 times of savings 1st loan and 4 times subsequent loans.

SHORT TERM LOAN

Loan granted to BOSA members but must be within multiplier and fully secured.
• Maximum loan amount is Kshs.300,000.
• Maximum repayment period is 24 months.
• Interest rate is 1% P/A Fixed.
Can run concurrently with BOSA Development/Normal loan

OWN SHARE

• An individual will be granted 80% of his/her share deposits. Granted after 3 months of saving.
• Interest rate is 1% per month on reducing balance.
